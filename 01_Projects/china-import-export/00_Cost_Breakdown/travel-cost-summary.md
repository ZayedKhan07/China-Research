## Summary

**Estimated travel cost range:**
- **USD:** $930 – $3,300
- **AED:** AED 3,400 – AED 12,100
- **INR:** ₹77,000 – ₹2,74,000

**Realistic mid-range budget for a first trip from India: ~$1,750 / ₹1,45,000**

This is the cost of getting to China from India, staying 8–10 days,
and returning — before any product sourcing or shipping costs.

Note: India → China flights are generally cheaper than US/UK → China,
which significantly reduces the total travel budget compared to
Western travelers.
